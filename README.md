# 🛒 Blinkit Sales Performance Dashboard

An interactive Business Intelligence dashboard designed to analyze and extract actionable insights from Blinkit's sales, customer ratings, and outlet performance data. This project breaks down sales trends based on item characteristics, outlet sizes, geographical tiers, and establishment timelines.

## 🔑 Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)

The dashboard tracks core business metrics to gauge overall operational health:
*   *Total Sales:* $1.20M (Overall revenue generated generated across all outlets)
*   *Average Sales:* $141 per transaction
*   *Number of Items Sold:* 8,523 unique items tracked in inventory
*   *Average Rating:* 3.9 / 5.0 (Customer satisfaction benchmark)

---

## 📈 Core Analytical Insights & Features

### 1. Outlet Breakdown & Performance
*   *Outlet Size Impact:* Evaluates revenue distribution across *High, Medium, and Small* outlet footprints. Medium outlets take a dominant share at 42% ($508K) of the market, followed closely by Small outlets at 37% ($445K).
*   *Location Strategy:* Analyzes revenue across *Tier 1, Tier 2, and Tier 3* cities. Tier 3 areas represent the highest revenue concentration block.
*   *Establishment Timeline:* A historical trend chart tracking outlet performance from 2010 onwards, highlighting a massive growth spike in *2015* yielding $205K in total sales.

### 2. Product & Inventory Diagnostics
*   *Fat Content Analysis:* Compares consumer preferences between *Low Fat (65%)* and *Regular (35%)* options, broken down further across various location tiers.
*   *Inventory by Item Type:* A comprehensive breakdown highlighting which categories drive transaction volume. Top-performing categories include *Fruits & Vegetables* and *Snack Foods*, while Seafood sits at the leanest inventory margin.

### 3. Granular Filter Panels
The dynamic side-dock enables targeted data drilling by:
*   *Outlet Size* (High, Medium, Small)
*   *Outlet Type* (Grocery Store, Supermarket Type 1, Supermarket Type 2, Supermarket Type 3)
